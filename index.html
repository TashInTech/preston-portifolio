Who I Am

Building at the intersection of
Engineering & Technology

I'm Preston Tash, a Civil Engineering student at Muteesa 1 Royal University and a self-taught full-stack web developer operating under the brand Tash In Tech. Based in Uganda, I specialise in building production-ready web platforms that solve real problems for businesses across the African market.

My development work goes beyond static pages — I build complete systems with Supabase databases, user authentication, Row Level Security, admin dashboards, and Flutterwave payment integration supporting MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, and card payments. I've successfully delivered full e-commerce platforms for clients in Uganda with live payment flows, order management, and content control panels.

On the engineering side, I work with AutoCAD Civil 3D on road design, longitudinal profiles, and production drawings — giving me a rare ability to think both like an engineer and a software builder.

I also create content in the AI & tech niche on TikTok (@tashintech), translating complex technology into accessible insights for a growing African audience.
